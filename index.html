About Venezia FC

Our History

Founded in 1907, Venezia Football Club has a rich history intertwined with the beautiful city of Venice. Our club has experienced the highs and lows of Italian football, from competing in Serie A to rebuilding in lower divisions.

The club's iconic green and orange colors represent the passion of our supporters and the beauty of Venice. Our home stadium, Stadio Pier Luigi Penzo, is one of the most unique venues in football, accessible by boat and offering stunning views of the Venetian lagoon.
