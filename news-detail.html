Venezia FC Defeats Fiorentina in Style

June 10, 2025

Venezia FC claimed a solid win over Fiorentina, showing sharp form ahead of the 2025/26 season. The team dominated possession and created several chances, with goals from Jay Idzes and Marin Å verko sealing the victory.

The match, held at Stadio Pier Luigi Penzo, saw a record attendance as fans cheered the team to victory. Coach Paolo Zanetti praised the squad's discipline and teamwork, stating, "This win is a testament to our hard work and unity."

With this result, Venezia FC looks forward to the upcoming fixtures with renewed confidence and determination.
